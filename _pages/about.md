Sofia Garcia is a third-year PhD candidate at the [ETH Zurich Robotics Systems Lab](https://rsl.ethz.ch/), where she works on robot learning and control. Her research focuses on reinforcement learning for legged locomotion, sim-to-real transfer, and dexterous manipulation.

She received her M.Sc. from the Technical University of Munich (TUM) in 2021, where she was awarded the DAAD Excellence Scholarship. Prior to that, she completed her undergraduate studies in Germany.

Sofia is currently a Research Intern at NVIDIA Robotics (April 2025 - Present). Previously, she was a Research Intern at the Boston Dynamics AI Institute (May 2023 - November 2023).
