This paper presents AnyTerrain, a reinforcement learning framework for training legged robots to traverse highly unstructured terrain. Our approach combines domain randomization with a carefully designed reward structure to produce robust locomotion policies that transfer from simulation to real-world environments. We demonstrate successful deployment on a quadruped robot across a variety of challenging terrains including rocky surfaces, steep slopes, and deformable ground.
