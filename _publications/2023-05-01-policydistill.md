This paper presents PolicyDistill, a method for compressing large locomotion control policies into smaller, more efficient networks without significant loss in performance. Using knowledge distillation techniques, we train compact student policies that mimic the behavior of larger teacher policies. Our results show that the distilled policies achieve comparable locomotion performance while requiring significantly fewer parameters and computational resources, making them suitable for deployment on resource-constrained robotic platforms.
